THE 5 FILTERING GATES
================================================================================

✅ GATE 1: ML ACCURACY CHECK
   Blocks trades when AI confidence < 75%
   Prevents low-accuracy model predictions from executing

✅ GATE 2: CONFIDENCE THRESHOLD CHECK
   Blocks trades when signal confidence < 80%
   Eliminates weak signals (below 80% = coin flip probability)

✅ GATE 3: MARKET STATE CHECK (ASIAN SESSION)
   Blocks trades during 22:00-08:00 London time
   Avoids unpredictable Asian market with low liquidity

✅ GATE 4: VOLATILITY CHECK
   Blocks trades when ATR too low (< 0.0005 * _Point)
   Prevents trading in choppy, range-bound markets

✅ GATE 5: SIGNAL QUALITY SCORE
   Blocks trades when combined quality < 75%
   Formula: (Confidence × 0.40) + (Score × 25) + (ML × 20) + (Vol × 15)

================================================================================
THROTTLING MECHANISM
================================================================================

Gate messages are throttled to prevent log spam:

Gate 1 (ML): Maximum 1 log every 30 seconds
Gate 2 (Conf): Maximum 1 log every 30 seconds
Gate 3 (Asian): Maximum 1 log every 60 seconds
Gate 4 (Vol): Maximum 1 log every 30 seconds
Gate 5 (Quality): Maximum 1 log every 30 seconds
Success: Maximum 1 success log every 60 seconds

This prevents the Experts tab from being flooded while still showing when
gates are active.

Example: If Gate 1 blocks 50 times in 10 minutes, you see the message once.
After 30s, if still blocking, you see it again.
