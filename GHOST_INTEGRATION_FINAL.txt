  1. Reads 60 most recent M1 candles
  2. Calculates Delta for each candle
  3. Sums CVD (persistent trend indicator)

  - g_ghost.delta     → Average delta over 60 bars
  - g_ghost.cvd       → Cumulative trend accumulation
  - g_ghost.buypct    → Buyer sentiment percentage
  - g_ghost.compass   → Direction indicator (0-7 octants)
