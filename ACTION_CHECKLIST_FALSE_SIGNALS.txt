  TROUBLESHOOTING
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

PROBLEM: Compilation fails with errors
──────────────────────────────────────
□ Check: Line number from error message
□ Read: The error message carefully
□ Action: Take screenshot and report exact error
□ Common fix: Verify SMC_Universal.mq5 is the latest version

PROBLEM: EA doesn't attach to chart
────────────────────────────────────
□ Check: Compilation was successful (0 errors)
□ Check: File exists in MQL5\Experts folder
□ Action: Restart MT5 completely
□ Try: Right-click chart → Expert Advisors → SMC_Universal again

PROBLEM: No gate messages appearing
───────────────────────────────────
□ Check: Experts tab open (Alt+L)
□ Check: EA attached to chart (check title bar)
□ Check: EnableTrading = true (check inputs)
□ Check: Wait for market signal (system may be idle)
□ Action: Scroll Experts tab to find [INIT] message (EA started)

PROBLEM: Too many trades still executing (gates not blocking)
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
□ Check: Which gate is blocking? Look at logs
□ Action: If no gate blocks, thresholds may be too lenient
□ Solution: Lower threshold values (75→70, 80→75, etc.)

PROBLEM: No trades executing at all (all signals blocked)
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
□ Check: Which gate blocks most? Look at logs
□ Action: Identify bottleneck gate
□ Solution: Lower that specific threshold (see Adjustments above)
