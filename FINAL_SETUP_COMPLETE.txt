TRADING LOGIC SUMMARY
================================================================================

Entry Conditions (ALL must be true):
   1. Divergence Score >= ConfluenceMin (3)
   2. Confidence >= 50%
   3. Positions < MaxPositions (3)
   4. Daily trades < MaxTradesPerDay (5)
   5. GOM level detected OR price near support/resistance
   6. AutoTrading enabled

Entry Execution:
   • Uses nearest GOM level if available
   • Otherwise uses current price
   • SL = Entry ± (ATR × SLMultiplier)
   • TP = Entry ± (ATR × TPMultiplier)
   • Lot size calculated based on risk%

Exit Conditions:
   • Stop Loss hit = Forced exit
   • Take Profit hit = Forced exit
   • Max hold time reached (10 bars = 10 hours H1)
   • 2% loss limit hit = Forced exit
   • Trailing stop activated (if UseTrailingStop = true)

PARAMETERS YOU CAN TUNE
================================================================================

More Conservative (Lower Risk):
   • DivThreshold: 0.18 → 0.20 (stricter divergence)
   • ConfluenceMin: 3 → 4 (higher score required)
   • RiskPercent: 1.2% → 0.8% (smaller lot size)
   • SLMultiplier: 1.4 → 1.8 (wider stop loss)

More Aggressive (Higher Signals):
   • DivThreshold: 0.18 → 0.15 (easier divergence)
   • ConfluenceMin: 3 → 2 (lower score required)
   • RiskPercent: 1.2% → 1.8% (larger lot size)
   • BarsForTouchCount: 150 → 300 (more GOM levels)

Better GOM Detection:
   • TouchZoneATRPercent: 25.0 → 30.0 (wider zone)
   • BarsForTouchCount: 150 → 250 (scan more bars)

TROUBLESHOOTING
================================================================================

Q: Dashboard not appearing
A: Check chart is updated (F5), wait 30 seconds, verify EA attached

Q: No GOM levels showing
A: Increase BarsForTouchCount to 250, lower DivThreshold to 0.15

Q: No divergence signals after 48 hours
A: Market conditions may not support divergence, try different pair/timeframe

Q: Robot attached but not trading
A: Check EnableAutoTrading = true, verify positions < 3, check daily trades < 5

Q: Orders rejected
A: Check spread not too wide, check sufficient margin, verify lot size
