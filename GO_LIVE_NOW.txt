================================================================================
QUICK FIXES
================================================================================

Q: Server crashes or won't start
A: Make sure websockets is installed:
   pip install websockets
   Then restart START_LIVE.bat

Q: Robot won't attach to chart
A: Check MT5 compilation output
   F7 in editor, look for errors
   Make sure Expert Advisors are enabled

Q: No GOM levels showing
A: Wait a few bars for scanning
   Check BarsForTouchCount setting
   Ensure EnableGOMEntryLevels = true

Q: Server responds but no signals
A: Divergence is probabilistic - may take hours to days
   Check for signals in MT5 Journal
   Verify settings in inputs
