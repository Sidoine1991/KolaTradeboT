Integration completed on DerivEAPro_v10.mq5:

1️⃣ GHOST_EstimateDelta()
   └─ Bookmap-proxy delta volume calculation
   └─ Formula: tick_volume × (2×(close-low)/(high-low) - 1)
   └─ Detects buyer vs seller pressure per candle

2️⃣ GHOST_CalcCVD()
   └─ Cumulative Volume Delta over 60-bar lookback
   └─ Indicates persistent trend accumulation
   └─ Updates every 5 seconds in polling cycle

3️⃣ GHOST_CalcSentiment()
   └─ Buy/Sell volume percentage analysis
   └─ 40-bar sentiment lookback
   └─ Thresholds: >65% bullish, <35% bearish

4️⃣ GHOST_CalcCompass()
   └─ EMA momentum direction (8 octants)
   └─ E/NE/N/NW = bullish (0°-135°)
   └─ W/SW/S/SE = bearish (180°-315°)

5️⃣ GHOST_Atan2Custom()
   └─ Helper function for angle conversion

6️⃣ PollGHOST()
   └─ Main handler called every 5 seconds
   └─ Integrated in OnTick() at line ~3579
   └─ Updates global SGhost g_ghost structure
